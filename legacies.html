LEGACIES 4

The Slug

1 As time proceeded and the church grew, the Musician Ko Stere put his trust in those whom he had befriended from outside the Church.

2 “Join our church, dear friends, we participate in merry game and competition in our community. And observe, dear friends; find amusement in the Worm and his splendor,” Ko Stere would tell his new recruits.

3 Of these new goers, most understood the way of the Church, finding their place in it comfortably and partaking in regular religious practice.

4 As Ko Stere spread to new lands in his quest to recruit more disciples, he recruited from many communities, pulling from his own land, and even from far reaches such as Mizkif’s kingdom.

5 And it was with the Mizkif Goggles that he obtained from this kingdom that he recruited from the Normans of the Norman Kingdom.

6 “My dear Norman friends, Trendan and Corey, I invite you to our church, a community of like-minded individuals who find great delight in our religious endeavors”

7 And so the Normans joined the church. But alas, they were met with a hidden skepticism on behalf of the Prophet soothsayer Howard.

8 For the Normans did not speak as the others spoke. Those who named the Worm in conversation were surprised to hear the Normans refer to Dallas as “this guy”.

9 “Is this guy for real?” the Norman Corey would ask. “Why do you guys talk about this guy so obsessively?” the Norman Trendan would ask.

10 “I sense the Normie Side of the force in them,” Howard warned to the Musician Ko Stere. “The pretentious, unfunny Slug Trendan I am most wary about. He has already expressed conflict with our religion,” Howard said.

11 But Ko Stere, with his Mizkif goggles, persuaded Howard of his belief in the Normans. “I like them,” Ko Stere argued, “Trendan is a valuable asset to our guild, he is so funny when he talks about Skyrim and makes animal crossing noises”

12 Howard nevertheless persisted in his reluctance, as the Slug insisted daily that the Church was unhealthily obsessed with Dallas.

13 Trendan knew this was the case because he was not obsessed or unhealthy in any manner. His healthy, plus-sized BMI allowed him the healthy energy to constantly be concerned about the behavior of other members of the church who were having fun in a private place of worship.

14 The Slug Trendan was so un-obsessed that he reached out to Howard’s wife, whom Trendan desired when she was unwed, in a private message to discuss the Church.

15 “Can I ask you something?” he inquired, with absolutely no Slug-like motivations. “How do you feel about the dallas galley stuff? The discord that's dedicated to basically harassing him. I'm just wondering your take, because there's few people that understand the extent of it”

16 Trendan knew he was a much better man for Howard's wife, for he was not only virtuous, but manly, as confident men only message their competition's wives and friends in secret.

17 The Slug continued his non-obsession by discussing the Prophet Howard with the Musician Ko Stere in private without ever once confronting the Prophet himself. “I don’t think he likes me and I cannot stand anyone not liking me. He seems so angry and not normally adjusted,” Trendan exclaimed to Ko Stere.

18 And by not realizing that Howard's anger was merely a bit and not serious, Trendan again displayed his exceptional intelligence. "Him yelling in games and bantering with his friends has always made me uncomfortable", Trendan expressed, again exhibiting very alpha-male behavior.

19 The Slug had grounds to make these claims against Howard since The Slug had a well-adjusted mental health and lacked any anger issues himself.

20 “I couldn’t stop thinking about killing myself, I’m so depressed, and I get so fucking angry when people don’t understand how smart I am. I need to prove my intelligence all the time by reciting economics I learned in college” the Slug explained to Ko Stere, “Howard is very abnormally adjusted however”.

21 As the healthily overweight Slug downed another alcoholic beverage, he made these claims to Ko Stere with a mind full of self awareness and no projection in the least.

22 Ko Stere soon realized that the Slug was devious and pathetic in his attempts and revealed Trendan’s schemes to Howard.

23 The Prophet Howard thanked Ko Stere for his loyalty and his defusal of the conspiracy. Thus, Howard removed the Slug and the remainder of the Normans from the church.

24 “We had a snake in the Church. A slug. This slug is malicious in its intent and is pathetic in its attempts” the Prophet announced as he burned the records of the Church to protect its longevity. Alas, the event came to be known as “The Purge”.

25 Today, the Church lives in seclusion and is exclusive in its membership. “We have learned our lesson today,” Ko Stere and Howard both agreed. “Henceforth, we must be wary of new recruits, and only welcome those with similar mind to our community”
